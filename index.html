Please try this game in your laptop or desktop PC.

3 2 1 - GO!

A game to test your reaction abilities like a race driver!

How to play:
  • Press the start button to start the game.
  • The lights will turn red in one second intervals.
  • When all lights are turned red, they will turn green in the next second.
  • After the lights turn green press the GO button to stop the timer.
  • If you press the button before the lights turn green the time will be invalidated.
  • Restart the game by pressing the chequered flag button.
